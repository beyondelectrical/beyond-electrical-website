Standards

Twelve years, one standard.

Beyond Electrical has never advertised. Every project we've taken on since 2014 came from someone who'd already seen the work firsthand — a builder, an architect, a past client. This is what we hold ourselves to in order to keep it that way.

We'd rather do fewer projects well than take on everything that comes our way.

Beyond Electrical was founded in Calgary in 2014 on a simple bet: that if the work was consistently excellent, we'd never need to chase it. Twelve years later, we've never run a marketing campaign — every project has come from a builder, architect, or past client who was willing to put their name behind ours.

That referral relationship is the whole business model, which means our incentives are straightforward. We're not paid to upsell, rush, or cut a corner that saves us an hour and costs the client a callback. We're paid to make sure the person who referred us looks good for having done it.
